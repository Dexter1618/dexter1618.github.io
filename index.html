I am currently a Data Scientist at miiCARE . I was previously an remote intern here, where I worked directly under the Founder & CEO, to create the foundational analytic and R&D work for the startup. Now, as a full-time core data scientist hired directly after graduation, I am continuing my foundational work for the company and pursuing more complex responsibilities involving analytics and novel research in deep learning and geriatrics to help the elderly and people with dementia live independent lives without the fear of being left alone or forgotten at times of personal injury, accidents and mishaps.

My current employment gives me an unique perspective to understand anomalous human behavior in old age or when afflicted with dementia. I consider this experience valuable to my personal journey towards the fabled lands of True AI, where intelligent behavior can be artificially generated, harnessed and explained simply. The current phase of "AI boom" is, in my opinion, misguided on the success of context-specific application of Supervised Deep Learning without generalization. I intend to break apart this mold of stale thinking and reinvent the idea of "learning" using Energy-based Approaches and Graph Neural Networks.
